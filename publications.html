About me

I am currently a Professor, Nanjing University. I was a research scientist in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, New York University Abu Dhabi and New York University Tandon School of Engineering. I did my Ph.D study in the Department of Computing, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang.
